Mo's Cart

Mo's Cart is a NYC food cart that specializes in breakfast food, sweet treats and coffee. This cart single handedly feeds the entire LaGuardia high school from morning to 7th period. The cart is often found with a line of 10 plus people who are late to their next class, but its worth it for a good morning pick-me-up.
